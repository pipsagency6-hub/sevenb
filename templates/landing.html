FAQ
Questions? Answered.
Where does the data come from?+
All economic data is sourced directly from FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data) — the same data used by central banks, hedge funds, and institutional traders. It's free, reliable, and updated automatically every hour.
How does the bias scoring work?+
Each market has a set of economic drivers (GDP, employment, inflation, etc.) with assigned weights. The model tracks the 3-month momentum of each indicator, applies the weights, and normalizes the result to a -100 to +100 score. Positive = bullish, negative = bearish.
What does the backtest show?+
The backtest replays the bias model month-by-month over the past 15 years. For each signal, it measures actual 1-month and 3-month forward returns. You see accuracy rates, win rates per bias bucket, equity curves, score distributions, and scatter plots.
How are the calculations automated?+
SevenB auto-fetches raw data from FRED every hour, computes YoY%, MoM%, net changes, trend scores, bias signals, and backtest metrics — all server-side. When new data is released (CPI, NFP, GDP, etc.), the dashboard updates within the next refresh cycle.
Which markets are covered?+
16 markets across equities (S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow), bonds (2Y, 10Y, 30Y Treasury), forex (EUR/USD, USD/JPY, GBP/USD, DXY), commodities (WTI, Brent, Gold), volatility (VIX), and credit (HY Spreads).
Can I share access with my team?+
Yes. The admin panel lets you create user accounts, monitor login activity, and disable or remove users at any time. Full control over who sees your data.
